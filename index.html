The Ultimate Golazo

Made with: Python

The Ultimate Golazo is a Python-based soccer simulation game inspired by FIFA, featuring realistic ball physics, player movements, and a challenging AI opponent. Utilizing object-oriented programming and CMU Graphics, the game offers strategic gameplay where players can control a team and compete against an AI opponent. Crosshair

Made with: APRIL Tag, Arduino, C++, GitHub, Linux, OpenCV, Python, Ubuntu, VSCode

Crosshair uses APRIL Tags and advanced computer vision to autonomously navigate and assemble components, minimizing human intervention. Using a modified ELEGOO Smart Robot Car Kit, we achieved precise and adaptive pathfinding. Despite challenges in motor control and vision accuracy, our solution demonstrated remarkable precision, consistently aligning objects within one inch. Future plans include integrating a Raspberry Pi for complete autonomy and expanding the technology to various robotic applications, including zero-gravity environments.
